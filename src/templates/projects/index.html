We are a technology based non profit association from France and our focus is on creating artificial intelligence based algorithms, programs and partner with researchers around the world to create customised workflows to address their bio image and data analysis needs.We use supercomputing resources to create deep learning models, provide annotated datasets for training the models and in this website you will find links to our articles, youtube channel and open source software and datasets. Please see our pricing model prior to contacting us for your requirements. Our codes are all open source and available on Github.

Fiji Plugins

We create Fiji plugins using imglib2, Scijava and jython macros for batch analysis of bio image datasets. Our plugins are available from Fiji update site: MTrack (Microtubule tracker, WizardofOZ), BTrack and TrackMate-Oneat. Click on this icon to discover more about...

We create annotated datasets and trained neural networks

Neural networks have to be trained to learn the data generating distribution, a task that requires carefully created annotated dataset, augmentation and hyper parameter optimization. All our trained models have undergone such quality control and are available as open source model files from zenodo.
